A Statement

Over the many years I have been recording bands and making records, it has always been an absolute passion of mine to keep my recordings feeling organic and sounding true to their original source. Rather than play the role of a producer or musical dictator, I am much more interested in documenting a time and place where you have full creative control and I am simply trying to make your vision come to life. That being said, although my initial approach is hands off, I don’t mind lending an opinion if requested.

Comfort is key. There are no set methods. Everyone approaches their own music and lives differently and that is why I have tried to be as flexible as possible when it comes to recording. If you want to record live as a band, that’s totally cool. If you want to track each instrument separately, we can do that. If you want to record the whole record with one mic submerged in a trash can full of water, I’m sure we can do that too. Unconventionality is tolerated here and highly encouraged. There is no right or wrong here and there is absolutely no authority but yourself.
